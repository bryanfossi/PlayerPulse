# PlayerPulse Tutorial Film Production Guide
### Step-by-Step Instructions for Creating a VEED.io Voiceover Tutorial Series

---

## OVERVIEW

This guide walks you through producing a complete, professional tutorial video series for PlayerPulse — the AI-powered college soccer recruiting platform. The series is designed to onboard new users quickly and help existing users get maximum value from every feature.

**Total Series:** 8 episodes  
**Recommended Format:** Screen recording + AI voiceover via VEED.io  
**Target Viewer:** Student athletes (ages 14–22) and their parents  
**Tone:** Encouraging, clear, and energetic — like a trusted older teammate explaining the ropes

---

## PART 1 — PRE-PRODUCTION SETUP

### Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before recording anything, assemble the following:

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| VEED.io | Voiceover generation, captions, editing | Free / Pro |
| Loom or OBS Studio | Screen recording | Free |
| PlayerPulse (demo account) | Live walkthrough footage | App login |
| Google Docs | Script writing | Free |
| Canva (optional) | Thumbnail creation | Free |

**Create a dedicated demo account** in PlayerPulse before recording. Populate it with realistic fake data:
- Player: "Alex Rivera," Class of 2027, Central Midfielder, 3.6 GPA
- 20–25 schools across all three tiers (Lock / Realistic / Reach)
- 4–5 contacts already logged
- 2 scholarship offers entered
- Onboarding fully complete

This ensures every screen you capture looks polished and believable.

---

### Step 2: Set Up VEED.io

1. Go to **https://veed.io** and create a free account (or sign in).
2. From the dashboard, click **"New Project."**
3. Select **"Upload"** to import your screen recording once it's ready, OR use **"Record"** to capture and produce in one step.
4. Navigate to **"Text to Speech"** in the left-hand toolbar — this is VEED.io's AI voiceover engine.
5. Choose your preferred AI voice:
   - **Recommended:** "Josh" or "Marcus" (male, confident, energetic) for a peer coaching tone
   - **Alternative:** "Aria" or "Sarah" (female, warm, clear) for a more professional feel
6. Set voice speed to **0.95x** — slightly slower than default for screen tutorial clarity.
7. Save your project settings as a template to reuse across all 8 episodes.

---

### Step 3: Plan Your Recording Environment

- Set your screen resolution to **1920×1080** before recording.
- Use Chrome browser. Zoom PlayerPulse to **90%** so more content fits on screen without scrolling.
- Hide browser bookmarks bar and all other tabs.
- Turn on **Do Not Disturb** mode to prevent notification popups.
- Set the PlayerPulse theme to **Dark Mode** (Settings page) — it reads better on video.
- Move your mouse deliberately and slowly. Viewers follow the cursor.

---

## PART 2 — EPISODE STRUCTURE & SCRIPTS

Each episode follows this format:
1. **Hook (0–10 sec):** One punchy statement about what this episode solves
2. **Overview (10–30 sec):** Tell them what you'll show them
3. **Walkthrough (bulk):** Step-by-step screen demo with narration
4. **Key Tips (last 30 sec):** 2–3 power-user tips
5. **CTA (final 10 sec):** What to do next / next episode teaser

## PART 3 — VEED.IO PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

### Step 4: Record Your Screen

**Using Loom (Recommended for beginners):**
1. Install the Loom Chrome extension.
2. Click the Loom icon → select "Screen Only."
3. Choose to record the current tab (PlayerPulse).
4. Record your screen demo WITHOUT speaking — use the script for AI voiceover.
5. Export as MP4 from Loom dashboard.

**Using OBS Studio (Recommended for quality):**
1. Open OBS → Sources → Add "Display Capture."
2. Add "Browser" source pointed at your PlayerPulse demo account.
3. Set output to 1920×1080, 30fps.
4. Record in silence (VEED.io adds voiceover after).
5. Export as MP4.

**Recording Tips:**
- Move the mouse slowly and purposefully to each element before narrating it.
- Pause on key screens for 2–3 seconds to give the voiceover room to breathe.
- For forms, fill them in slowly — don't type at full speed.
- Record each episode in a single continuous take when possible.

---

### Step 5: Import to VEED.io

1. Open VEED.io → New Project → Upload your MP4.
2. Wait for upload and processing (1–3 minutes per episode).
3. Trim the start and end to remove any dead frames using the timeline scrubber.
4. If you made a mistake mid-recording, split the clip at that point and delete the bad section.

---

### Step 6: Add AI Voiceover

1. In the VEED.io timeline, click **"Text to Speech"** in the left panel.
2. Paste the full episode script into the text box.
3. Select your AI voice (consistent across all 8 episodes).
4. Click **"Generate Audio."**
5. VEED.io produces a complete voiceover track and places it on the timeline.
6. Listen through with your screen recording playing — check that the narration aligns with what's on screen.

**Syncing tips:**
- If the voiceover runs ahead of the action: split the audio track and add a short pause (silence gap) at the relevant moment.
- If the voiceover falls behind: trim the screen recording clip at quiet moments to tighten the pacing.
- VEED.io's timeline lets you drag audio clips left and right — use this to align narration to each screen transition.

### Step 7: Add Captions

5. Keep captions on for all episodes — many viewers watch without sound.
